Peanut Butter Cookies

Peanut butter cookies are beloved treats known for their rich peanut flavor and distinctive crisscross pattern on top. These cookies typically have a golden-brown appearance and a slightly crispy exterior with a soft, chewy interior. The key ingredient, of course, is peanut butter, which infuses the cookies with a nutty and slightly salty taste. Commonly found ingredients in peanut butter cookies include all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, brown sugar, butter, eggs, baking soda, and a pinch of salt. To create the classic crisscross pattern, the dough is often flattened with a fork before baking. Peanut butter cookies have a comforting and nostalgic quality, making them a cherished favorite for peanut enthusiasts and cookie lovers alike.

Recipe

Ingredients

For the Cookie Dough:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup peanut butter (smooth or crunchy, your choice)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 & 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 175Â°C and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, peanut butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy, which should take about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Then, stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt (optional if using salted butter).
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the peanut butter mixture and mix until just combined.
  6. Roll the cookies into balls, and place onto the prepared trays. If you prefer, you can use a fork to create the classic crisscross pattern on top of each cookie.
  7. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden (the cookies will continue to set as they cool).
  8. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
